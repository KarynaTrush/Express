Dance Styles

Explore the variety of dance styles we offer and find the one that speaks to you.

Ballet

Ballet is the foundation of all dance forms, known for its grace, precision, and demanding technique. Our ballet classes focus on building strength, flexibility, and poise for dancers of all levels.

Contemporary

Blending elements of ballet, jazz, and modern dance, contemporary emphasizes freedom of movement and emotional expression, creating a versatile style perfect for exploring personal creativity.

Jazz

Jazz dance is vibrant, energetic, and often performed to upbeat music. Our classes cover classic jazz techniques, helping dancers improve rhythm, coordination, and performance quality.

Hip-Hop

Known for its expressive style and street origins, hip-hop includes elements like popping, locking, and breaking. Our hip-hop classes explore choreography and freestyle, embracing all skill levels.

Latin Dance

Latin dance includes styles such as salsa, bachata, and rumba, filled with rhythm and passion. Learn to connect with the music and your partner in these social and lively dance forms.

Modern Dance

Modern dance breaks away from the structure of classical ballet, focusing on raw movement and emotions. Classes explore unique techniques like floor work and expressive gestures.

Ballroom Dance

Ballroom dance offers elegance and formality, including styles like waltz, tango, and foxtrot. Learn to move gracefully with a partner and master the art of partnered dancing.

Tap Dance

Tap dance combines rhythm and footwork to create percussive sounds. Our classes focus on building speed, timing, and precision, allowing dancers to create music with their movements.

Afro Dance

Afro dance celebrates African heritage with energetic movements and rhythms. This style is full of life and brings a unique cultural experience, inviting everyone to connect with music deeply.
